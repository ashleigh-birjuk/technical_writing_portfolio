How to Create and Manage Tasks

Learn how to create and manage tasks in Productivity Plus to stay organized and efficient.

Before you begin, ensure that you have installed Productivity Plus and have an active account.

  1. Create a new task:
    1. Open Productivity Plus and navigate to the task management section.
    2. Click the "New Task" button.
    3. Enter the task name in the "Task Name" field.
    4. Optionally, add a description, due date, and assign labels.
    5. Click "Save" to create the task.
  2. Manage existing tasks:
    1. To edit a task, click on the task name in the task list.
    2. Make the necessary changes and click "Save".
    3. To delete a task, click the "Delete" button next to the task name and confirm the deletion.
    4. To mark a task as complete, check the box next to the task name.

By following these steps, you can efficiently create and manage tasks in Productivity Plus, ensuring that you stay organized and on top of your responsibilities.
